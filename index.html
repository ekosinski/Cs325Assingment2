Nerdy Dating Sim

  • High Concept: Players will get to enjoy a funny comedy dating simulator aimed toward the nerd stereotype.
  • Player Experience Goals: Each player will get to enjoy a sense of comedy as they try to date the girl in front of them. Each of the questions has hilarious responses, that players should enjoy every round whether they get them right or wrong.
  • Genre: Nerdy Dating Sim is a spice-of-life dating simulator parody. It spends its gameplay making fun of the dating genre.
  • Game play: In Nerdy Dating Sim five questions will be posted. The next question will only appear when the question before it is answered. Each question has one right answer and two wrong answers. On the left side of the screen you will see a girls face. Everytime you answer a question correctly she will get happier. Everytime you answer a question incorrectly she gets sadder. In the upper corner of the screen there will be a Ask Out button. If you press this button and the girl is happy enough you win. Otherwise you lose the game.
  • Prototype goal: The main goal of this prototype is to not only test phaser's ability to detect mouse clicks on specific objects, but also to test the ability of objects to spawn or despawn off of triggers.
  • Features: As stated before the great feature of Nerdy Dating Sim is it's great comedy. All questions posted in this game were taken by a popularity poll, and have better odds than others for dating women. Players will definitly gain some dating advice through Nerdy Dating Sim.
  • Setting/Premise: Nerdy Dating Sim takes place in its own world. The player creates their own experience as they try to date a girl using nerdy pick up lines.
  • Story: You are a student taking an Introduction to Game Programming. With Valentines day coming up you feel its time to make sure your prepared to ask a girl out. Social interaction isn't your strong suite so you try out your skills on a simulator you found online. Let's see how prepared you are.
  • Target Audience: Nerdy Dating Sim appeases to teenagers under the stereotype of being a nerd.
  • Play time: Nerdy Dating Sim should take about 5 to 40 seconds.
  • Strategies: Players can dicuss with friends which questions seem mostly likley to be correct, especially friends who have dating experience.
  • Prototype Assets: Most images were made in either illustrator or downloaded from google images.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: No phaser games were truly copied from Nerdy Dating Sim.
